About Chermside

North of Brisbane, Chermside is one of the most fun places to visit in Australia.
Only 20 minutes from CBD, frequent transports, and the biggest Westfield in Australia.
This guide is telling you about things to do at Chermside, mainly for those who are retired and want to spend some time with friends !

Eat

There are many cafes and restaurants around Chermside. Good to have a lunch/dinner party with your friends

Fun

Have fun with your friends at places like Chermside Bowls Club and Kedron Wavell Services Club

Shop

You do not need to go to city to shop. At Chermside, the biggest Westfield in Australia offers everything to you.

Relax

Relaxing in nature, like 7th Brigade park, or visit beauty therapies at shopping center.
