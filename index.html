About Chermside

North of Brisbane, Chermside is one of the most fun places to visit in Australia. Only 20 minutes from CBD, frequent transports, and the biggest Westfield in Australia.
This guide is telling you about things to do at Chermside, mainly for those who are retired and want to spend some time with friends !

Eat

There are many cafes and restaurants around Chermside. It's the best place to have a lunch or dinner with your friends!

Fun

Have fun with your friends at Chermside Bowls Club and Kedron Wavell Services Club

Shop

Chermside's biggest Westfield shopping center maybe better than CBD

Relax

Relaxing in nature or body treatment at a beauty therapy
