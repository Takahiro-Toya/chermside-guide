About Chermside

North of Brisbane, Chermside is one of the most fun places to visit in Brisbane. Only 20 minutes from CBD, frequent transports, and the biggest Westfield in Australia.
This guide is telling you about things to do at Chermside, mainly for those who are retired and want to spend some time with friends !
