Tosakan Thai

  • A small middle class authentic Thai restaurant

    Tosakan Thai is a middle class small Thai restaurant. It is a small business but always busy with local people during dinner time. A welcoming and laxuary atomosphere will guarantee your experience and you will definitely have a great time with your friends! Their plates are all authentic, and Thai alcohol is available, so if you need SINGHA beer!

  • Traditional & Contemporary Thai food

    You may be familiar with most of Thai food because Thai restaurants are usually popular options in Australia. But at Tosakan Thai, you will see something unique, because they are mixing Authentic Thai foods and what Australian people love!

  • Fresh food every day

    It is a middle class restaurant, and their food is prepared daily and so always fresh! The Chef & Co-Owner Thanapon Vutthavanich cares quality of every ingredient. Their ambitous of serving finest & fresh Thai food has been successful and so this restaurant has been popular among locals.
