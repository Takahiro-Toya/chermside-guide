Chermside Bowls Club

  • What is Chermside Bowls Club?

    If you want to enjoy talking with your friends over playing sports, Chermside Bowls Club may be a good choice. It is also a good idea to play bowls as an exercise and keep your body active!

  • Facilities

    Chermside Bowls Club's facilities include Lawn Bowls yard, UBET machines, Gaming room such as Dart Board, and restaurants and cafes. As this place has gaming and pub spaces in addition to bowls yard, you are able to enjoy your whole day with your friends! Staffs are also friendly and their attitude is welcoming, so if you are not good at playing bowls and other games, they are going to help you! The lawn bowls yard is outside without roof, so it maybe too hot during summer season. Playing bowls is an good exercise, but you need to make sure not to get heatstroke!

  • Restaurants & Pub

    Chermside Bowls Club has its own restaurant and pub. The restaurant's menus are Pizza, Steak, Burgers, Fish&Chip, and so on. They are all pretty reasonable but tasty!. If you are tired of playing bowls, then just take a seat and drink a glass of beer! Eating and Drinking while watching sports or horse racing is also a good idea.
