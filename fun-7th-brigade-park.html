7th Brigade Park

Sitting just next to Westfield Chermside, the biggest park in Chermside is 7th Brigade park...

BBQ with your friends!

Just like other parks around Brisbane, 7th Brigade park also has BBQ grills.
Why is the 7th Brigade park's BBQ is better than other places ? Because it is a quiet place!
Unlike other BBQ places around Brisbane, no people here so you can easily find a place to gather with your friends and no disrupttion!
